How do I play?

- You will be presented 16 cards all faced down.

- You must then select 1 card which it will flip to its face picture (All sports activities). It is your job to find its matching pair within the remaining cards.

- Once you have found the matching pair, that pair will remain visible and you must continue until all pairs (8 in total) are found. If it doesnt match the cards will flip back over.

- There is a timer and total amount of selections counted if you wish to make it a little more competitve for yourself.

Have fun!!
